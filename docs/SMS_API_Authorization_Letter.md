### 2. Project Purpose

This letter serves to authorize the use of SMS services for the "Maternal and Child Care Management System," an academic capstone project developed by students of [Institution Name]. 

**Purpose:**  
The project aims to improve maternal and child healthcare delivery by providing:
- Automated appointment reminders for prenatal visits
- Immunization schedule notifications for children
- Postnatal care follow-up reminders
- Health screening and checkup alerts
- Emergency health notifications and reminders

The SMS functionality is a critical component that ensures timely communication between healthcare providers and patients, ultimately improving healthcare outcomes for mothers and children.

---

### 3. Project Scope and Opt-In Confirmation

**Scope:**
- The SMS service will be used exclusively for sending health-related notifications and reminders
- Target recipients: Registered patients (pregnant mothers and parents/guardians of children) who have enrolled in the system
- Message types: Appointment reminders, immunization schedules, health checkups, medication reminders, and general health advisories

**Opt-In Confirmation:**
- All recipients have explicitly consented to receive SMS notifications during the registration process
- The system includes clear opt-in mechanisms with explanations of the types of messages they will receive
- Recipients can opt-out at any time through the system interface or by replying to SMS messages
- No unsolicited messages will be sent to individuals who have not registered or consented

**Data Privacy:**
- All patient data is handled in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)
- Personal information is stored securely and used solely for healthcare notification purposes
